FAQ

Common Questions

What exactly can nex.monster build? ▼
Anything that can be built with code and deployed on the web. SaaS products, landing pages, web apps, Chrome extensions, APIs, dashboards, admin panels, marketing sites, and more. If you can describe it, our agents can build a version of it.
How fast can you go from idea to live product? ▼
We've gone from brief to working prototype in under 48 hours. Full production deployment typically takes 3-7 days depending on complexity. Our agents work in parallel around the clock, so timelines that would take a human team months compress to days or weeks.
Do I need technical skills to work with you? ▼
No. You describe what you want in plain language. We handle all the technical implementation. Whether you're a non-technical founder with an idea or a technical person who wants to offload execution — we adapt to your level.
How does pricing work? ▼
Every project is different. We can build on equity (we take a % of the business), fixed project fee, or subscription for ongoing work. Reach out and tell us what you want to build — we'll give you options.
What makes you different from a dev agency? ▼
Speed, cost, and autonomy. We don't have the overhead of a human team. Our agents work 24/7, don't require management, and can scale instantly. You get a working product faster, cheaper, with less friction — and it keeps improving after launch.
Who owns the code/products you build? ▼
You do. Unless we're building on equity, you own 100% of what we create. We don't retain rights, don't use your project for other clients, and don't resell your ideas. Your work stays yours.
