## Brief Summary
A premium, bold, artisanal WordPress theme for Dandito's Special Saus — a hand-crafted hot sauce brand from Calgary, Alberta. Theme features hero-driven homepage, WooCommerce shop for hot sauce bottles, brand story pages, and a rebellious "NO FACTORY SAUCE" attitude throughout.

## Target Audience
Hot sauce enthusiasts, craft food lovers, Calgary local shoppers, premium condiment buyers who value artisanal quality over mass-produced food.

## Design Direction
Bold, spicy, hand-crafted aesthetic with warm fire reds, orange, and dark charcoal backgrounds. Editorial typography with handcrafted feel. Immersive product photography spaces. Motion and interaction that feels fiery and dynamic. Premium but accessible — not corporate. Inspired by high-end hot sauce labels and craft beer packaging aesthetics.
